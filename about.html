Phoenix Plants Lifesaver Pvt. Ltd.

We started our Journey in 2019 with a vision to make an transforming impact in agriculture society. We observed the problems and issues of basic Agriculture System. We address challenge of farming in typical rural areas and starts working to finding the solutions of there issues from grass-roots level. At first we marked the challenges of farmers which are stopping them to uplift their farming culture. After this we address the problems of whole retail channel which are forcing them to stuck in traditional business practices, which are totally outdated and have a lower financial statics. Then we acknowledge the network across the country and found that we are in a huge trouble in terms of productions of core fertilizers and distribution statices across the country. We collected all these facts and issues by working with farmer, retailer and traders communities of multiple geographical areas. After all of these observation we stabilised the organisation Phoenix Plants Lifesaver Pvt. Ltd. with a vision to make a transforming and developing impact in this Agriculture Society.
